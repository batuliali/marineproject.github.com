About Us

Our mission is to address the impact of water pollution on marine life, the economy, and tourism. Learn how we’re making a difference.

Our team is dedicated to raising awareness about the severe consequences of ocean pollution. We aim to educate, advocate, and drive action to protect marine ecosystems.

  • Highlighting the effects of pollution on marine life.
  • Addressing economic impacts on local fishermen.
  • Promoting sustainable tourism practices.

We believe in taking proactive steps to mitigate water pollution and protect our oceans. Through research, community engagement, and advocacy, we work towards a cleaner, healthier marine environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about how ocean pollution impacts marine life and what you can do to help.

How does plastic pollution affect marine life?

Plastic pollution harms marine animals through ingestion and entanglement. Many species mistake plastic for food, leading to injury or death. Entanglement in plastic waste can also cause drowning or hinder an animal's ability to move freely.

What are microplastics, and why are they a problem?

Microplastics are tiny plastic particles that result from the breakdown of larger plastics. They are difficult to remove from water and can be ingested by marine organisms, entering the food chain and potentially affecting human health.

What can I do to help reduce ocean pollution?

You can help by reducing plastic use, participating in beach cleanups, supporting policies that limit pollution, and educating others about the importance of protecting our oceans.
